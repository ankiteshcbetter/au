Our Project

AU Skills Academy

AU Skills Academy is a project that intakes young peers from an economically poor background and trains them for a job in the BFSI Sector. Along with theory classes, we prepare them with the help of multiple supporting soft-skills such as - communication, presentation, leadership, teamwork, Information Technology and so on.
