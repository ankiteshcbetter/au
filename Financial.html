Financial & Digital Literacy

Having taken banking to the remotest of places, we were determined to introduce financial inclusion in the country. In order to accomplish that, we knew we had to activate and evolve a new culture. Banking needed to instill faith amongst people, which could break the shackles of traditional, and local, money-lending schemes. We observed that banking could be an intimidating concept for people in these places, and using extensive jargon could prove to go against us. We created nukkad-nataks, plays, songs, etc. for people to enjoy and, simultaneously, understand both the complexity and benefits of banking better through relatable human stories. Where hard facts could have failed, the art of storytelling prevailed. What better way to connect and communicate than song and dance!

Our Project

Financial Literacy Camps

The Financial Literacy Camps aim at reaching people in remote areas as well as unbanked locations and educate them about various financial services and tools made available to them by the banking infrastructure. Innovative methods of teaching & learning are implemented to achieve maximum impact.

Across Rajasthan

  • Financial Literacy Camps are organized in rural Unbanked locations.
  • They are used to educate people about the Financial and Banking Services and Tools.
  • The camps build trust about banks & banking amongst these people.
  • They introduce members of the unbanked communities to Banking and Financial Services.
