Sports For Development

Sports play an integral role in the development of a well-rounded personality. Respecting one’s opponent, team awareness, leadership skills and learning the spirit of the game are some of those life skills that are big benefits from repeatedly playing any game. In order to build an emotionally and physically robust nation, we encourage kids to go out and play. At least 30-35 million children between age 6 to 14 do not go to school. The reasons may vary from they being in a marginalized society, to unavailability of educational facilities where they live, or maybe they just don’t want to. Millions of children living in relatively neglected areas of the society (slums & so on), tend to turn to vices or fall out of school and end up doing small-time jobs in unorganized sectors. They lack basic life skills like communication, general awareness, reading & writing abilities, etc. Our MD & CEO of AU Bank, Shri. Sanjay Agarwal, himself being an avid sports person, with an experience of playing in the field for many years has come to believe that so many of the life & business skills that he now possesses, he learned from sports itself. Sports has proved to be a brilliant alternative education method, on hundreds of instances. Sports being used to teach many life skills, as well as academic and general education about life and the world, is what Sports for Development is all about.

I want to be a sports coach in future, but I didn’t have the coaches who could guide me, tell me my mistakes, and help me to improve my skills. But after joining Sports for Development programme I am getting all the things free of cost, which I couldn’t afford ever, says Krishna.

Our Project

AU Bank Sports Village

The AU Bank Sports Village is a sports intervention program, that strives to bring about a subtle social change in the rural community of Mahapura. By using sports to bring together kids from surrounding communities, we’re driving a unique program that aids in the kids’ physical, mental and emotional development.
