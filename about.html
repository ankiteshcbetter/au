ABOUT AU FOUNDATION

India has a humongous population of 400 million who have joined the workforce in the country! And yet, social inequality is still a problem, that we as a country are struggling with. So widespread is its reach that it causes much anxiety in the hearts of those with a vision of a thriving nation. We at AU Foundation firmly believe every Indian deserves equal opportunity, whether one is living in an urban setting or rural humble parts of the country. Our country has the immense advantage of diversity. We not only need to tap into the infinite potential of individuals who are forming and transforming our nation and its future, every day. We increasingly have realised that we also need to lend a helping hand to those who should be a part of this transformative force of India.

AU Foundation is a young social development organization*. Its mission is to enable willing and talented Indians, to lead a purposeful life. We recognise that certain individuals are disadvantaged by a severe lack of opportunities, specialized training and infrastructural requirements. Our aim is to fulfill their needs by bridging such gaps.

The foundation has been set up by the fast growing AU Small Finance Bank. We aim to utilise our experience in banking and finance to overcome widespread social challenges in the country. It is our humble endeavor to do so through a highly skilled, well-trained approach to issues such as skill education and employment in India.

As one of the pioneers in small finance banking in India, we have witnessed and realized the importance of an all-round participation of the people for the evolution of an industry. In doing so, our mission met with huge challenges, particularly a restrictive environment against banking. These were not just intellectual or emotional, but also social, and even geographical in nature. All in all, deep rooted societal practices put up challenges for us on every frontier. We saw this experience as strong feedback to effectively design an all-inclusive model for the social development of all parts of India.

We believe that the best way for us to demonstrate our Social Responsibility was by finding avenues from our existing organizational ability. After much deliberation, we chose three distinct avenues to explore.
